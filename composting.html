Composting

In our future cities, composting will be the norm. Nowadays, composting is rather rare simply because people are not entirely used to the idea, or because of other issues such as barriers against starting to compost (the education on the subject, a motivation from peers to partake in composting, or lack of a compost machine). However, this will become more commonplace, as recycling is, over time.

Motivations: Our primary ways of getting people to begin composting are the following:
  • Currently, it is unlikely that people live in neighborhoods where composting is the norm.
  • We will encourage composting amongst prevelant members of various communities to encourage the behavior to spread throughout the communities.
  • We will make it easy as possible to compost, by providing equipment necessary to people that wish to partake.
We also will have a reward system for composting. Those that choose to partake in the compost network, which will be the compost equivalent of the recycling network, can receive rewards by random raffle. This is explained more in detail in the rewards system page. Other systems can be developed to encourage use through our rewards system, such as recycling, as well as limiting water usage.
