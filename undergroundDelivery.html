Delivery

Underground delivery will be commonplace in our futuristic cities. This will be a very exciting change that we begin to see as these new cities are developed. It will be much more efficient than current delivery methods.

Currently, there is a lot of waste of cardboard. Online shopping is a large cause of this. Companies such as Amazon have standardized box sizes so even the smallest item is sometimes packaged in a large box, wasting space.

We will have a air pressure powered underground delivery in our future cities to get things from Point A to Point B without a cardboard box. These will be installed in towns and cities alongside sewer systems that already exist and will use air pressure to move the items throughout. This is a very exciting development and will make delivery even quicker than our already rapid delivery systems, but more importantly we will preventDefault(); cardboard waste as well as carbon dioxide emissions from vehicle delivery.
